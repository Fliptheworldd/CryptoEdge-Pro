Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

TL;DR: CryptoEdge Pro is 100% local software. Your API keys, trade data, and personal information never leave your computer. We don't collect, store, or transmit anything. This privacy policy exists for legal compliance only.

1. Introduction

CryptoEdge Pro ("we", "our", "the software") is a desktop application for cryptocurrency trading with AI-powered analysis. We take your privacy seriously.

Core Principle: All data stays on your machine. We have no servers, no cloud storage, no tracking.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Computer

  • API Keys: Binance, OpenAI, Claude (encrypted with AES-256)
  • Trade History: Timestamp, pair, entry/exit, PnL
  • Settings: Trading mode, risk parameters, language preference
  • Master Password Hash: SHA-256 hash (not the password itself)

Storage Location: SQLite database in your app's local data folder.

We NEVER: Upload this data to our servers. It's yours, forever.

2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • ‚ùå No usage analytics
  • ‚ùå No telemetry
  • ‚ùå No crash reports (unless you explicitly send them)
  • ‚ùå No IP addresses
  • ‚ùå No device fingerprints
  • ‚ùå No cookies (this is not a web app)

3. How Data is Used

3.1 API Keys

Used exclusively to:

  • Connect to Binance for trading operations
  • Send market analysis requests to OpenAI/Claude

Never: Shared with third parties, logged, or transmitted to our servers.

3.2 Trade Data

Stored locally to:

  • Display your trade history
  • Calculate performance metrics (win rate, profit factor)
  • Generate analytics charts

Never: Uploaded, sold, or used for marketing.

3.3 AI Analysis

When you enable AI trading:

  • Market data (price, volume, indicators) is sent to OpenAI/Claude APIs
  • This happens via your API key (direct connection)
  • We do not proxy or log these requests

Your Responsibility: Review OpenAI/Anthropic privacy policies if you use their services.

5. Data Security

5.1 Encryption

  • Master Password: SHA-256 hashing (one-way, cannot be reversed)
  • Database: Stored in OS-protected app data folder

  • Export: Use "Export to CSV" feature in the app
  • Opt-Out: No tracking to opt out of (we don't track)

Data Controller: YOU are the data controller. We are the software provider.
